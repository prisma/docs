If you want to explore Prisma Postgres without leaving your browser, you can try it out the via Google's [Project IDX](https://idx.google.com/), a fully-fledged online IDE:

1. Open the [**Databases**](https://idx.google.com/templates?category=databases) templates.
1. Select **Prisma** and choose **Prisma Postgres** as your database.
1. Click the **Create** button create your IDX workspace.
1. Wait until the IDX workspace has fully initialized and then follow the instructions in the README to explore the main features or Prisma Postgres.
