Platform Console enables you to integrate the latest Prisma Data Platform products into your application

- [Accelerate](/data-platform/accelerate/what-is-accelerate): An automated, global cache and serverless connection pool that speeds up your Prisma Client queries
- [Pulse](/data-platform/pulse/what-is-pulse): A managed Change Data Capture (CDC) service that lets you subscribe to realtime events in your database using Prisma Client
